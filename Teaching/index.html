Contracted by The Open University to facilitate the Massive Open Online Course (MOOC) "In The Night Sky: Orion" on the FutureLearn platform. Responsibilities included moderating comments, answering queries on the content, and encouraging participation and collaboration between participants.

Outreach

North York Moors National Park Dark Skies Festival
Two day event held at Hunley Hotel & Gold Club, Teesside catered for the general public and local schools. During this event I manned a pop-up stand demonstrating the OpenScience Observatories, and communicating the exciting solar system science ongoing from partners all around the world.
OpenLearn: Asteroid Day
As part of wider 'Asteroid Day' celebrations, I published an article on the OpenLearn platform to communicate my PhD research project on asteroid observations & brightness variations.
