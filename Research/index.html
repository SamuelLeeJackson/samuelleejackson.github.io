PhD Research

My doctoral work at The Open University focused on characterising asteroid spin and surface properties using small-aperture robotic telescopes. I collected over 1,000 hours of observations with the OpenScience Observatories to determine physical properties of 18 asteroids, and investigated the viewing geometry dependence of near-Earth asteroid phase curves.

The thesis is available open access via the Open Research Online repository.
